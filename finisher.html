IT'S TECHNICALLY A FRUIT, I GUESS

INGREDIENTS (check the list off as you waddle around the store)

PREPARATION

  1. Whisk heavy whipping cream until you reach soft peaks
  2. blend the strawberries, avocados and condensed milk using only a gilded whisk cast from gold of the ancient city of Tenochtitlan as to not disturb the avocados
  3. Mix everything into the whipped cream and add powdered sugar
  4. Place the mix into the avocado shells and add a thin slice of strawberry on top
  5. As an option, put leftovers in a bowl with slices of strawberry. I don't know why you would have any leftovers though. Eat that shit up.
  6. Freeze for two hours, then enjoy!
