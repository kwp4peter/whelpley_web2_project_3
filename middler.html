  1. For the Garlic Sauce: In a small/medium jar, mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, oregano, and chili flakes together, shake it well, and save it in refrigerator for later (For leftover garlic sauce, refrigerate it and it lasts for up to one week).
  2. For the Pizza: Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees.

  4. Bake it in the oven for 25-30 minutes until the cheese melts and the crust become golden brown.
