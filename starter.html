OCTUPLE THE FAT CONTENT OF A SALAD IN 5 MINUTES

INGREDIENTS (check the list off as you waddle around the store)

PREPARATION

  1. Cook bacon until crispy. Drain on a paper towel, then set aside. Skip this step if you're not a coward.
  2. Place chopped lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, AVOCADOS OH MY LORD, and bacon in a large bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, salt, and lemon juice to form the dressing.
  4. Toss the salad with dressing and separate the avocado from the salad. Throw rest of salad out, you don't need it where you're going.
  5. Enjoy!
